# Troubleshooting

---

## App cannot locate my school

**Symptoms:** School not found after entering the ID.

**Steps to resolve:**

1. Double-check that the ID you entered is correct — do not add spaces before or after the school ID.
2. If the ID starts with a zero (`0`) and the school cannot be found, try entering the ID again omitting the leading zero.
3. If the problem persists, contact your focal point at the UNICEF Country Office. The ID you entered might not be registered in Giga's systems yet.

---

## Running an old version / missed the update prompt

**Symptoms:** App shows an outdated version; no update pop-up appeared, or it was dismissed.

**Steps to update:**

1. Visit [meter.giga.global](https://meter.giga.global/)
2. Download and install the latest version

The new installation will automatically overwrite the older version. Your school registration will be preserved — you do not need to register again.

---

## Registered with the wrong school ID

**Steps to correct:**

1. Open Giga Meter
2. Go to **Settings**
3. Click **Logout**
4. Enter your school ID and click **Logout** again to confirm
5. You will be taken back to the registration screen — follow the steps from [Step 11 of the Installation Guide](../installation/installation-guide.md#step-11--accept-the-policies) to re-register with the correct school ID

> [!WARNING]
> Logging out removes the registration for **all users on this device**. No more internet quality measurements will be recorded for the previously registered school ID until re-registration is complete.

After re-registering, all Windows users on the device will be automatically updated. To verify, switch to other user accounts and open Giga Meter to confirm they show the correct school ID. If not, restart the device or app and register again.

---

## Country not detected correctly

**Symptoms:** App detects the wrong country during registration.

**Steps to resolve:**

- Select the correct country from the dropdown and click **OK**, then **Confirm**.
- If a warning message still appears after selecting the correct country, this may be due to a VPN or your IP address signaling a different location. You can proceed by clicking **Confirm**.
- If the message says **"Giga Meter is not available in [country]"**, verify your country selection is correct. If it persists, contact your designated Giga focal point at UNICEF.

---

## Tests are failing or not running

**Symptoms:** Tests show as failed, or no data appears on Giga Maps.

**Checklist:**

- [ ] Is the device turned on and **not** in Sleep mode?
- [ ] Is the device connected to the school's internet?
- [ ] Is it within the measurement window (8 AM – 8 PM local time)?
- [ ] Does the school have a working internet connection?

If the test fails repeatedly, take a screenshot of the error and share it with the administrator who guided your installation.

---

## Other issues

For any issue not covered above: take a screenshot or copy the error message and send it to your focal point at the Ministry or at the UNICEF Country Office (as instructed). They will get in touch with Giga to provide support.
