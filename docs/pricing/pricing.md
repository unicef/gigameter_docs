# Pricing

{% hint style="warning" %}
**Restricted** — This page is for government partners and institutional procurement only. Do not share publicly.
{% endhint %}

Giga Meter pricing follows a cost-recovery model. Fees cover infrastructure, operations, and support — not profit. There are no per-school, per-user, or per-measurement fees. Any surplus is reinvested into the product.

***

## Plans at a glance

{% columns %}
{% column %}
### Tier 1 — Self Serve

Annual subscription

Application and analytics platform hosted and maintained by Giga, with asynchronous deployment support. For governments with strong in-country IT capacity.

**Measurement Client + Analytics**\
$92,000 / year

**Customer Success**\
$23,000 (one-time)
{% endcolumn %}

{% column %}
### Tier 2 — Supported

Annual subscription

Everything in Tier 1, plus a dedicated installation campaign success manager embedded for the first three months of deployment.

**Measurement Client + Analytics**\
$92,000 / year

**Customer Success**\
$63,000 (one-time)
{% endcolumn %}

{% column %}
### Tier 3 — Self Hosted

Annual subscription

Government hosts and manages its own Giga Meter infrastructure. Giga provides the open-source codebase, knowledge base, and access to a dedicated support manager.

Contact us for pricing.
{% endcolumn %}
